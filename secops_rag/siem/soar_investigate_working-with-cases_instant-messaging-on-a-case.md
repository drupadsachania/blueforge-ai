Use Case Chat to collaborate in real time
Supported in:
Google secops
SOAR
This document explains how to use the
Case Chat
feature in Google Security Operations
  to collaborate in real time. Case Chat provides a secure and convenient,
  built-in messaging experience that lets you and other team members
  (with access to a case) communicate directly within the platform.
Available features include:
Mention specific users or SOC roles to trigger email notifications. When
  you mention a role, all users assigned to that role are notified.
Pin important messages to the Case Wall, to make them easier to find and reference.
Send a message in Case Chat
To chat with other members within a case, follow these steps:
Click
Chat
to open the side drawer.
To mention someone, enter
alternate_email
alternate_email
and the user's name or role.
Optional: Attach one or more files.
Click
Send
.
Opotional: To pin your message to the
Case Wall
, hold the pointer over the message and click
Pin to Case Wall
.
