Standard upgrade of Remote Agents
Supported in:
Google secops
SOAR
This document explains how to perform a
standard
upgrade of Remote Agents.
The standard upgrade procedure may take up to five minutes.
For information on performing a
major
upgrade of Remote Agents, which requires extra steps, see:
Perform a major upgrade of a Docker image
Perform a major upgrade using installer for RHEL
Perform a major upgrade using installer for CentOS
Perform a major upgrade of a Podman image
Upgrade Remote Agent (1.4.2 and above)
Go to
Soar Settings
>
Advanced
>
Remote Agents
.
Click
Update Available
.
Click
info
Info
to confirm the updated version.
Upgrade Remote Agent (1.4.1 and below)
Go to
Settings
>
Advanced
>
Remote Agents
.
Double-click the agent.
Disable the agent and then refresh the page.
After the status is disabled, double-click the agent to enable it.
When the agent is live, click
info
to confirm the updated  version.
