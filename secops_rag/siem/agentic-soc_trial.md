Google Agentic SOC trial details
The Triage and Investigation Agent (TIN) is an agentic AI feature for Google Security Operations that helps automate security investigations. This document describes the terms, usage limits, and duration for the no-cost trial of TIN.
For more information on TIN, see
Use Triage and Investigation Agent to investigate alerts
.
Eligibility
The Google Agentic SOC trial is available for customers with an active subscription to one of the following Google SecOps packages:
Enterprise
Enterprise Plus
Google Unified Security (GUS)
Tokens are granted on a per-subscription basis and may not be pooled, banked, or transferred between your different eligible subscriptions.
Trial period
This trial starts
April 1, 2026
and automatically ends for all customers on
June 30, 2026
, unless Google provides written notice of an extension.
Trial usage limits
Your use of TIN during the trial is measured in
trial runs
. A trial run is equivalent to a single investigation performed by TIN, either automatically or manually.
Your organization's usage is subject to the following limits:
Customer Tier
Total Hourly Limit
Limit Breakdown
Enterprise
10 trial runs
Up to 5 automatic and 5 manual runs
Enterprise Plus & GUS
20 trial runs
Up to 10 automatic and 10 manual runs
If your organization's use reaches or exceeds these limits, you will have to wait for one hour for the limits to refresh again.
After the trial ends
When the trial period ends on June 30, 2026, the following occurs:
For Enterprise Plus and Google Unified Security Customers
: Your trial access ends and automatically transitions to the complimentary Security Token allotment included with your base subscription. To purchase capacity beyond your complimentary allotment, you must purchase a paid subscription to the Google SecOps Security Tokens SKU.
For Enterprise Customers
: Your access to TIN ends. You retain access to historical detections from investigations run during the trial, but you can't run new investigations. To continue using TIN after the trial, you must purchase a paid subscription to the Google SecOps Security Tokens SKU.
