In
Your Workdesk
, the
My Cases
tab contains cases assigned to you,
your role, or cases where you're mentioned.
View case details
To view more details about a specific case, click
View Details
next to 
the case. This opens the
View Details
side drawer, which displays 
additional case details, including the highlighted information. If you need 
further details or want to configure a service-level agreement (SLA), 
click
Go to case
.
Access SLAs for alerts
To view SLAs for alerts for a specific case, hold the pointer over
SLA for Alerts
.
