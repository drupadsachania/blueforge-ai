Define a landing page
Supported in:
Google secops
SOAR
Administrators can define a specific page for Google Security Operations users to see after they sign in to the platform.
Users are directed to their assigned landing page based on their permission group.
Permission groups are determined by the user's Identity Provider (IdP) or email
group, depending on their authentication method.
This makes sure that users are directed to the most relevant section of the platform. Users can return to their landing page at any time by clicking
home
Home
.
