Use predefined widgets in playbook views
Supported in:
Google secops
SOAR
Predefined widgets are ready-made widgets linked to actions in integrations
from the Content Hub. Created by
Google SecOps experts, these widgets provide specific information for
actions, saving time on customizing and building a playbook view.
After you download the integration, the actions appear as usual
when creating a playbook, with special icons that indicate predefined widgets.
You can use these widgets in existing playbooks or when creating new ones.
Build a new playbook view with predefined widgets
Go to
Google SecOps Content Hub
>
Response Integrations
and select
VirusTotal
.
Click
Read More
. Some of the VirusTotal actions come with predefined widgets:
Download and configure the VirusTotal integration as
required.
Go to
Response
>
Playbooks
and explore how to use an
action with a predefined widget.
Create a new playbook, open the
Step Selector
and drag the
VirusTotal Enrich hash
action.
Click
Add View
and enter a name (contains VirusTotal widgets) and choose
a role. In this example, leave the checkbox selected to include
predefined widgets. You can choose to opt out or remove them later after creating
the view.
After you create the view, the widget is automatically
added to the view. You can only edit these options in this
widget:
Name
,
Description
,
Conditions
.
After the playbook is attached to an alert, it appears on the
Playbook
tab of the  case alert view.
Add predefined widgets to an existing playbook
Locate the required playbook and open the
Step Selection
.
Drag the
VirusTotal Enrich URL
action into the playbook, and click
Save
.
Open the view, where the predefined widget appears in the
Predefined
column.
Drag it into the view.
