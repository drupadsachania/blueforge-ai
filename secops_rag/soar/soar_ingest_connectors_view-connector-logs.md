Supported in:

SOAR
Connector logs help you identify and troubleshoot potential issues with
custom or commercial connectors.
To view connector logs, follow these steps:
Go to
SOAR Settings
>
Ingestion
>
Connectors
.
Select the relevant connector and click the
Logs
tab.
Click the
Enable Log Collection
toggle to on and select the required level
from the
Log level
menu.
Click
autorenew
Refresh
to display the newly collected logs on the page.
After three hours, the
Enable Log Collection
toggle turns off automatically, and 
the timer stops. You can choose to disable it manually at any time. The
logs remain visible on the page until the next time you enable log collection.
Each log entry has an icon that identifies the issue with a short description.
Click
View More
to see the full log details.
