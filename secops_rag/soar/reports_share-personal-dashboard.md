Share a personal dashboard
Supported in:
Google secops
SIEM
This document explains how to share a personal dashboard with other members of your team.
Personal Dashboards
are visible only to you.
Shared Dashboards
are
visible to all members of the team who can view dashboards in Google Security Operations SIEM.
Go to
Personal Dashboards
in the left navigation.
Select the dashboard you want to share, and then click the
more_vert
menu next to the dashboard name.
Select
Copy to Shared
. A copy of the dashboard is created under the
Shared Dashboards
section in the left navigation.
