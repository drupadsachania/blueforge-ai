Google Threat Intelligence (GTI) score overview
Supported in:
Google secops
SIEM
The Google Threat Intelligence (GTI) score system helps Google Security Operations 
prioritize and address the most significant security threats. GTI score calculates 
threat levels for various indicators, including, domains, IP addresses, file hashes and URLs.
The GTI score is a numeric representation (from 0-100) of an indicator's potential
impact if detected in an environment. The scoring system combines Mandiant
Threat Intelligence data with analyst expertise
to determine the score.
