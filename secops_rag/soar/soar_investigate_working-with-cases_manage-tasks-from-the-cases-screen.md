Track tasks in cases
Supported in:
Google secops
SOAR
This document explains how to create and manage case-specific or general
  tasks, and assign them to a SOC role or team member. You can perform these
  actions directly from
Cases
>
Case Wall
or
Your Workdesk
>
My Tasks
.
Add a task from the Case Wall
To add a task from the Case Wall, follow these steps:
In the case
Overview
tab, click
Case Task
.
In the
Add Task
dialog, enter the title, SOC role or team member, task content, and due date.
Click
Save
.
Assign the SOC role or team member
To change the SOC role or team member assigned to the case, follow these steps:
From the Case Wall, click
Tasks
.
Select the task you want to assign.
Click the list next to
Assigned to:
.
Select the required SOC role or team member. The assignment is updated.
Close a task
To close a task, follow these steps:
From the Case Wall, click
Tasks
.
Select the task you want to close.
Click
Mark as done
.
Enter a comment in the
Comment
field and then click
Send
.
For information about using tags to categorize cases and alerts, see
Manage tags in cases and alerts
.
